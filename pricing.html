Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Free Plan?

The Free Plan includes consultation, basic planning, and guidance on your project requirements. It's perfect for discussing your needs and understanding our approach before committing to a paid plan.

How long does development take?

Development time varies by plan and complexity. Basic websites typically take 1-2 weeks, Standard plans 2-3 weeks, and Premium plans 3-4 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and major credit cards. Payment plans are available for larger projects. A 50% deposit is required to start development.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes! All plans include initial maintenance. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally.

Do you provide hosting?

Yes, hosting is included in Basic, Standard, and Premium plans for the first year. We handle all technical aspects so you can focus on your business.

Do I get a dedicated account manager?

Premium plan clients get a dedicated account manager for personalized service. All clients have direct access to our support team throughout the project.
